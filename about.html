Who We Are

Health and Environment Solutions Organization (HESO) is a non-governmental organization based in Arusha, Tanzania, committed to promoting sustainable development. We address key challenges in health, environment, agriculture, and water resources to improve the well-being of communities and ensure a healthier, more sustainable future.

Core Values

  • Integrity – We uphold honesty and transparency in all our actions.
  • Sustainability – We focus on long-term solutions that preserve resources for future generations.
  • Equity – We advocate for fairness and inclusivity in all our initiatives.
  • Community Empowerment – We believe in the strength and potential of local communities to drive change.
  • Innovation – We embrace creative approaches to solve emerging challenges.

Our Commitment

At HESO, we are committed to building a Tanzania where every citizen can live a healthy life in a clean, safe, and thriving environment. Our interdisciplinary approach brings together public health, environmental science, community organizing, and advocacy to tackle today’s challenges and prepare for tomorrow’s.

We believe that with the right knowledge, tools, and partnerships, every community can be a champion of sustainable development.

Our Principle Objectives

To promote Public Health and Disease Prevention

Enhance community health through awareness campaigns, access to health services, and the promotion of preventive practices targeting both communicable and non-communicable diseases.

To support Environmental Conservation and Climate Resilience

Engage in activities that promote the protection of natural resources, climate adaptation, and mitigation of harmful environmental effects.

To advance Community Education and Empowerment

Educate and empower individuals and communities—especially women, youth, and vulnerable populations—on sustainable practices related to health, hygiene, waste management, and environmental stewardship.

To facilitate Research and Innovation

Conduct and support locally relevant research that informs health and environmental policies, technologies, and practices suited to Tanzania's unique context.

To strengthen Policy Advocacy and Stakeholder Engagement

Collaborate with government institutions, civil society organizations, and international partners to advocate for effective policies, resource mobilization, and integrated action on health and environmental issues.

To enhance Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) Services

Improve access to clean water, sanitation facilities, and hygiene education in underserved and rural areas to reduce disease burden and environmental degradation.

To promote Sustainable Livelihoods and a Green Economy

Support income-generating activities and green technologies that are environmentally friendly and improve community resilience and economic independence.

Encourage Eco-Entrepreneurship and Skills Development

Promote training programs and initiatives that equip community members with skills in sustainable practices, such as organic farming, renewable energy, and eco-friendly crafts, enabling them to create green businesses and job opportunities.
