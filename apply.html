Apply Online

Join our mission to improve health and protect the environment in Tanzania by applying to be part of the Health and Environment Solutions Organization (HESO). Whether you're interested in volunteering, pursuing an internship, or seeking employment, we're excited to connect with passionate individuals who are ready to make a difference.

Application Opportunities

We welcome applications for the following roles
Role Description
Volunteering Contribute your time and skills to support health and environmental projects at local and national levels. Both short- and long-term placements are available.
Internship Gain hands-on experience in areas such as environmental research, public health education, climate action, or WASH programs. Ideal for university students or recent graduates.
Staff Recruitment Explore current job openings and apply to become part of our dynamic and purpose-driven team.
