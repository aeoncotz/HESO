Stories of Real Change

See how communities across Tanzania are building healthier, more resilient futures through HESO's integrated programs — as of 30th , December 2025.

October 2025
Menstrual Health Empowerment

Reusable sanitary kits and hygiene education reached 120+ girls at a rural secondary school, reducing stigma and absenteeism.

87 percent no longer miss school due to menstruation
Peer support groups formed among students and mothers.

Thematic Area: Health

November 2025
Community Climate Resilience Workshop

Open-air session under trees engaged 80+ community members in climate adaptation, sustainable land use, and environmental protection strategies.

Participants gained practical knowledge on drought resilience
Plans formed for local tree planting and water conservation initiatives.

Thematic Area: Environment & Climate Resilience

August 2025
Maternal & Child Health Outreach

Community clinic session provided postnatal care, nutrition advice, and child health checks for 40+ mothers and infants in a rural area.

Improved early detection of malnutrition
Families received essential supplements and follow-up referrals.

Thematic Area: Health & Nutrition

July 2025
Community Health Awareness Session

Interactive workshop in a local classroom engaged 60+ residents on preventive health, nutrition, and disease awareness.

Increased knowledge of hygiene practices
Participants committed to sharing information in their households.

Thematic Area: Health

September 2025
Nutrition Support for Mothers & Infants

HESO distributed essential infant formula and nutritional supplements to vulnerable families, reaching dozens of mothers and under-5 children.

Improved access to safe nutrition
Reduced risk of malnutrition in young children.

Thematic Area: Agriculture & Nutrition

October 2025
Lake Shore Conservation Champions

Community leaders showcased restored lake shore areas after HESO-led reforestation and water protection efforts in the region.

Improved ecosystem health
Reduced erosion and better water quality reported by local users.

Thematic Area: Environment & Climate Resilience

November 2025
Climate-Smart Avocado Farming Success

Smallholder farmers trained in sustainable techniques now harvest healthy avocados from drought-resistant grafted trees.

Increased yields and income
Improved soil health through intercropping and mulching.

Thematic Area: Agriculture & Nutrition

December 2025
Women's Empowerment & Awareness Session

Maasai women participated in an open-air workshop receiving educational materials on health, hygiene, and community rights.

Enhanced knowledge and confidence
Materials distributed for ongoing household and peer education.

Thematic Area: Health & Community Empowerment

November 2025
Sustainable Avocado Orchard Growth

Smallholder farmers applied climate-smart techniques to establish young avocado trees, promoting fruit production and soil conservation.

Healthy tree growth observed
Expected future income boost from diversified farming.

Thematic Area: Agriculture & Nutrition
