Latest Updates & Stories

Explore our latest activities, success stories, and community-driven impact across Tanzania in health, environment, and sustainable development.

Climate Resilience in Action

Through the Tanzania Climate Change Forum (TCCF), we recently hosted a series of community dialogues and gender-responsive workshops aimed at building climate adaptation capacity among women farmers in Arusha and Morogoro.

Read More
WASH Project Expansion

HESO has installed 15 new water purification units in rural communities, improving access to clean drinking water and hygiene education. These projects are supported by monthly donor contributions and local partnerships.

Read More
Health Outreach Week

In collaboration with district health officers, HESO conducted a mobile health clinic outreach across Singida region, offering malaria screening, immunizations, and maternal care services to over 1,200 residents.

Read More
Youth Empowerment & Education

As part of our community education goals, HESO launched sustainability clubs in 10 secondary schools, offering climate literacy workshops and student-led environmental action projects.

Read More
National Clean Water Summit

HESO participated in the National Clean Water Summit in Dodoma, advocating for improved sanitation infrastructure and showcasing our low-cost water filtration models for rural households.

Global Partnership Announcement

HESO has officially partnered with international NGOs to scale up our Green Economy initiative, supporting sustainable agriculture and eco-enterprises in over 5 districts.
