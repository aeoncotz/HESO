Our Programs

At HESO, our programs are designed to tackle the interconnected challenges of public health, environmental degradation, and community vulnerability. Through innovative and community-based approaches, we implement integrated programs that foster resilient health systems, protect natural resources, and empower communities with the knowledge and tools to build a better future.

Each of our programs reflects our commitment to sustainability, equity, and action. Explore our core areas of work below:
01

Public Health Promotion & Disease Prevention

    Program Goal

    To improve the health and well-being of Tanzanian communities through preventive healthcare, health education, and access to basic health services.


    Key Activities
  • Health outreach campaigns focused on communicable and non-communicable diseases (e.g., malaria, HIV/AIDS, diabetes, hypertension).
  • Health screenings and mobile clinics in rural and underserved areas.
  • Maternal and child health promotion, including nutrition and vaccination support.
  • Behavior change communication on hygiene, nutrition, and lifestyle-related conditions.
  • Collaborations with public health institutions to strengthen local health systems.

    • Impact Focus

    Reduce disease burden, improve health literacy, and enhance access to primary health care across Tanzania.

02

Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH)

    Program Goal

    To ensure access to safe drinking water, improved sanitation, and good hygiene practices as a foundation for community health and dignity.


    Key Activities
  • Construction and rehabilitation of water points, wells, and rainwater harvesting systems.
  • Promotion of community-led total sanitation (CLTS) and elimination of open defecation.
  • Hygiene education in schools and communities, including menstrual hygiene management.
  • Distribution of water purification technologies and handwashing facilities.
  • Capacity-building for local water committees and WASH governance.

    • Impact Focus

    Prevent waterborne diseases, reduce environmental contamination, and improve overall hygiene and sanitation in marginalized areas.

03

Sustainable Agriculture & Nutrition

    Program Goal

    To promote climate-smart agricultural practices and enhance food and nutrition security among vulnerable populations.


    Key Activities
  • Training smallholder farmers in climate-resilient farming techniques and organic practices.
  • Promoting crop diversification and indigenous, nutrient-rich foods.
  • Supporting school and community gardens to improve child nutrition.
  • Linking agriculture with local markets and cooperative development.
  • Nutrition education, especially for pregnant women, caregivers, and young children.

    • Impact Focus

    Increase agricultural productivity, strengthen food systems, and improve community nutrition through sustainable farming.

04

Environmental Conservation & Climate Resilience

    Program Goal

    To protect natural ecosystems and build community resilience to the impacts of climate change through education, restoration, and climate action.


    Key Activities
  • Tree planting and reforestation programs in degraded landscapes and watersheds.
  • Environmental education and awareness campaigns on climate change, pollution, and deforestation.
  • Promoting the use of clean energy technologies such as improved cookstoves and solar lighting.
  • Participatory climate adaptation projects (e.g., flood control, soil erosion prevention).
  • Engagement in national and global climate advocacy and dialogues.

    • Impact Focus

    Restore natural habitats, reduce emissions, and prepare communities for climate shocks and environmental threats.

05

    Key Activities
  • Training programs on waste management, nutrition, hygiene, and environmental stewardship.
  • Life skills and leadership development workshops for youth and women.

  • Peer education initiatives and community radio outreach.

06

Research, Innovation & Knowledge Sharing

    Program Goal

    To generate and apply local knowledge that informs evidence-based policies and scalable interventions in health and environmental sectors.


    Key Activities
  • Conducting operational research and baseline surveys.
  • Piloting and testing health and environmental technologies suitable for rural communities.
  • Publishing knowledge products (briefs, reports, toolkits) and facilitating academic partnerships.
  • Organizing conferences, forums, and webinars to share findings and innovations.
  • Supporting youth-led research and innovation initiatives.

    • Impact Focus

    Bridge the gap between research and action while supporting innovation and data-driven decision-making.

07

Policy Advocacy & Stakeholder Engagement

    Program Goal

    To influence policies, mobilize resources, and strengthen partnerships for inclusive and integrated health-environment solutions.


    Key Activities
  • Engaging in national policy dialogues and technical working groups.
  • Mobilizing stakeholders through roundtables and community consultations.
  • Producing policy briefs, position statements, and advocacy campaigns.
  • Facilitating civil society engagement in local governance and planning.
  • Participating in regional and international networks to elevate Tanzanian voices.

    • Impact Focus

    Strengthen cross-sector collaboration, improve policy environments, and amplify community concerns in public decision-making.

08

Sustainable Livelihoods & Green Economy

    Program Goal

    To promote eco-friendly economic opportunities that support household income and preserve environmental integrity.


    Key Activities
  • Training communities on sustainable income-generating activities (e.g., beekeeping, eco-tourism, composting, recycling).
  • Micro-enterprise development and financial literacy training.
  • Linking green products to markets and developing cooperative business models.
  • Supporting women's and youth entrepreneurship in the green economy.
  • Promoting environmental certification and eco-labelling initiatives.

    • Impact Focus

    Create jobs, reduce poverty, and transition communities toward sustainable development.
