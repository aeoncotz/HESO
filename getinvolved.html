Be a Force for Change

Health and environmental challenges in Tanzania cannot be tackled by one organization alone — they require the passion, talents, and collective action of people like you. Whether you're a student, professional, local resident, international supporter, or an organization, there’s a place for you in our movement. Join HESO and help us create a healthier, greener, and more just future for all Tanzanians.

Volunteer With Us

Make a direct impact by giving your time and skills. We welcome volunteers from all walks of life to join us in various community projects, health campaigns, environmental cleanups, school programs, and organizational tasks. You can choose from: Short-Term Volunteering: Great for individuals or groups who want to contribute during school breaks, holidays, or specific events. Long-Term Volunteering: Ideal for those who want to engage deeply with our work over several weeks or months. Local and International Volunteers: Whether you’re from Tanzania or overseas, we have opportunities tailored to your interests and background. Your time can transform lives — and your own.

Partner With Us

Collaborate with HESO to multiply your impact. We actively seek partnerships with: Local and international NGOs working in health, environment, youth, education, or climate change Academic institutions interested in joint research, internships, or training programs Private companies and social enterprises seeking to fulfill their corporate social responsibility (CSR) goals or support community innovation Government agencies looking to implement inclusive, data-driven development programs Together, we can scale up what works and innovate for greater sustainability. Let’s build solutions together.

Fundraise for HESO

Your creativity can power real change. Do you want to support our work, but can’t be on the ground? Start a fundraising campaign from wherever you are! Here’s how: Host a charity walk, benefit concert, or community clean-up fundraiser Run an online campaign via social media or crowdfunding platforms Celebrate your birthday, graduation, or special event by raising money for health and environment causes Engage your workplace, faith group, or school in a “Give for Good” campaign Every shilling counts — and every effort inspires.

Internships & Field Placements

Learn, grow, and contribute through practical experience. We offer internship and field placement opportunities for: University students studying public health, environmental sciences, development, agriculture, or communications Recent graduates seeking hands-on experience in community work, program design, or research International students and gap-year travelers looking to make meaningful contributions in Tanzania You’ll work alongside professionals, engage directly with communities, and gain invaluable skills that prepare you for your career. Let your learning leave a legacy.
